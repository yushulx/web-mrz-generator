# Privacy Policy for MRZ Generator

**Last Updated: November 4, 2025**

## Data Collection

This extension **does NOT collect, store, or transmit any user data**.

## What We Don't Do

- ❌ No data collection
- ❌ No tracking or analytics
- ❌ No cookies
- ❌ No personal information storage
- ❌ No data transmission to external servers
- ❌ No third-party services

## How the Extension Works

1. **Local Processing**: All MRZ code generation happens locally in your browser
2. **User Input**: Any information you enter (name, document number, etc.) is processed locally and immediately discarded after document generation
3. **Temporary Data**: Generated MRZ codes and images exist only in memory during your session
4. **Offline Capability**: The extension works completely offline using locally bundled libraries

## Permissions

This extension requires **NO special permissions**.

- No activeTab permission
- No clipboard access
- No storage access
- No network access

## Data Storage

The extension does not use:
- Local storage
- Session storage
- IndexedDB
- Cookies
- Cache (except for the extension's own files)

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not connect to any external servers or third-party services. All functionality is self-contained within the extension.

## Generated Content

- All MRZ codes and document images are generated locally on your device
- When you download generated images, they are saved directly to your device
- We have no access to any content you generate

## Children's Privacy

The extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any data at all, the extension is safe for all age groups.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be updated in the extension's store listing.
